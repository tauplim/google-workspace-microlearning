# Facilitator Guide: Google Workspace with AI
## Trainer-Led Microlearning Course

**Version:** Draft 1
**Duration:** 8 sessions × 30 minutes
**Audience:** Beginners with basic computer literacy
**Max participants:** 12 (for workshop quality)

---

## Facilitator Preparation Checklist

### Pre-Course Setup
- [ ] Test Google Workspace demo accounts for all participants
- [ ] Verify Gemini AI features are enabled in your organization
- [ ] Pre-create shared Drive folder structure for exercises
- [ ] Load slide decks on presentation screen
- [ ] Print participant workbooks (or confirm digital access)
- [ ] Set up timer visible to facilitator (use phone or second screen)
- [ ] Prepare observation checklists for each module

### Room Setup
- [ ] U-shape or small groups for workshop phases
- [ ] Power outlets accessible for all participants
- [ ] Projector/screen for trainer demo (dual-screen recommended)
- [ ] WiFi credentials posted

### Pre-Session Ritual (5 minutes before each session)
1. Launch demo environment
2. Display current module slide on screen
3. Queue up timer
4. Test microphone (if virtual)
5. Confirm all participants have Workspace access

## Session Facilitation Framework

### Opening Protocol (3 minutes)

**Say:** "Welcome to Module [X]. Today we're focusing on [learning objective]. By the end of this session, you'll be able to [specific success criteria]."

**Show:** Slide with learning objective and success criteria.

**Do:** Share the compression artifact preview (what they'll take away).

---

### Learn Phase Delivery (9 minutes)

**Minute 3-6: Concept Introduction**
- Keep declarative - "Here's what it is and why it matters"
- Use analogy or real-world metaphor
- Show ONE clear example

**Minute 6-10: Live Demonstration**
- Think-aloud while performing the action
- Pause at decision points: "Now I'm going to... watch what happens"
- Repeat key step twice if complex

**Minute 10-12: Key Principle Highlight**
- Transfer: "Now YOU try this"
- State the pattern they'll replicate

---

### Consolidate Phase Delivery (15 minutes)

**Minute 12-22: Guided Workshop**
- Distribute task card from workbook
- Circulate room, check progress every 3 minutes
- Use observation checklist to identify struggling participants
- Call out helpers: "If you finish, help your neighbor"

**Minute 22-25: Peer Exercise**
- Pair participants for peer review
- One shares screen, other observes and notes
- Rotate roles

**Minute 25-27: Share-back & Debrief**
- Ask 1-2 volunteers to demonstrate
- Highlight what worked, address challenges
- Connect to compression artifact

---

### Compress & Close Protocol (3 minutes)

**Minute 27-28: Pattern Recap**
- "What's the ONE thing to remember about [topic]?"
- Get response from 2-3 participants

**Minute 28-29: Compression Artifact**
- Distribute or display the artifact
- "Take 30 seconds to add your name and one personalization"

**Minute 29-30: Bridge to Next Session**
- "Next module builds on this by adding [X]. Before next session, try [brief exercise]."

## Module 1: Foundation - Google Workspace with AI

**Learning Objective:** Navigate the Google Workspace ecosystem and initiate AI assistance in core applications.

**Success Criteria:** Describe 5 core Workspace apps and start Gemini in at least 3 of them.

### Session Timeline

| Time | Phase | Activity |
|------|-------|----------|
| 0:00 | Open | Welcome, objectives, success criteria |
| 0:03 | Learn | Ecosystem overview + Gemini integration demo |
| 0:12 | Consolidate | Navigate apps, locate Gemini in each |
| 0:27 | Compress | Hotkey card, bridge to Module 2 |

### Talking Points

**Ecosystem Overview (3 min)**
"Google Workspace isn't just email. It's five core apps that talk to each other: Gmail for communication, Calendar for scheduling, Drive for storage, Docs/Sheets/Slides for creation, and Meet for collaboration. Gemini AI is now embedded in all of them, acting as your 24/7 productivity assistant."

**Gemini Demo (4 min)**
Demonstrate in sequence:
1. **Gmail**: Show Gemini summarize an email thread
2. **Docs**: Draft a paragraph with Gemini
3. **Sheets**: Generate formulas with Gemini
4. **Drive**: Search with natural language

**Workshop Task (10 min)**
"Open your workbook to Module 1 exercise. Your task: Open each core app, find the Gemini icon, and send one AI prompt in each. I'll be circulating to help."

### Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Gemini not visible | Check organization AI settings; use gemini.google.com as fallback |
| Participant stuck on login | Pre-stage demo accounts; have IT contact ready |
| Fast finishers | Challenge: "Try the advanced prompt examples in your workbook" |

### Compression Artifact
**"Gemini Hotkeys" Quick Reference Card**
```
Gemini Shortcuts in Google Workspace:
• Gmail: Click Gemini sparkle icon in compose
• Docs: Tools → Gemini or Ctrl+Shift+M
• Sheets: Click Gemini icon in toolbar
• Drive: Use gemini.google.com for file search
• Meet: Gemini in meeting notes (post-meeting)
```

## Module 2: Collaboration with AI

**Learning Objective:** Use real-time collaboration features with AI assistance for co-authoring and feedback.

**Success Criteria:** Co-edit a document and use AI to generate, respond to, or resolve comments.

### Session Timeline

| Time | Phase | Activity |
|------|-------|----------|
| 0:00 | Open | Bridge from M1, objectives |
| 0:03 | Learn | Collaboration patterns + AI commenting demo |
| 0:12 | Consolidate | Co-author document with Gemini suggestions |
| 0:27 | Compress | Collaboration checklist, bridge to M3 |

### Talking Points

**Collaboration Pattern (3 min)**
"The shift from 'send and wait' to 'share and collaborate' is fundamental. With AI assistance, you don't just share documents - you share thinking partners. Watch how Gemini can suggest comments, generate responses, and resolve threads automatically."

**Live Demo (4 min)**
1. Create new Doc, enable editing for participant
2. Show "Suggesting mode" vs "Editing mode"1. **Gmail** - Universal entry point; email workflows with AI
2. **Google Calendar** - Scheduling and meeting 
3. Add AI-generated comment suggestion
4. Demonstrate AI "Help me write a reply"

**Workshop Task (10 min)**
"Pair up with your neighbor. Share access to a document. One person adds a comment, the other uses Gemini to generate a thoughtful response. Then use AI to resolve the thread."

### Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Sharing permissions blocked | Use "Anyone with link can edit" for demo |
| AI comment generation slow | Pre-generate one example as backup demo |
| Participant works alone | Create triad groups instead of pairs |

## Module 4: AI-Powered Research

**Learning Objective:** Synthesize information from multiple sources into structured summaries using Gemini.

**Success Criteria:** Use Gemini to synthesize information from multiple sources into a structured summary.

### Session Timeline

| Time | Phase | Activity |
|------|-------|----------|
| 0:00 | Open | Bridge from M3 (spaced retrieval), objectives |
| 0:03 | Learn | Research synthesis + Gemini extraction demo |
| 0:12 | Consolidate | Research task with 3 source materials |
| 0:27 | Compress | Research workflow, bridge to M5 |

### Talking Points

**Research with AI (3 min)**
"Gemini doesn't just generate text - it can read and synthesize. You can paste in articles, reports, or data, and Gemini will extract patterns, compare viewpoints, and summarize key insights in seconds."

**Demo (4 min)**
1. Open 3 sample articles in browser tabs
2. Copy/paste key sections into Gemini
3. Prompt: "Compare and contrast these sources. What do they agree on? Where do they differ?"
4. Generate bullet-point synthesis

**Workshop Task (10 min)**
"Work with your provided source materials. Extract key data points and use Gemini to generate a one-page research brief. Format: problem statement, 3 key findings, conclusion."

## Module 8: AI Integration Final Project

**Learning Objective:** Demonstrate integrated competencies by building a complete AI-powered workflow.

**Success Criteria:** Deliver a completed workflow integrating at least 3 Google Workspace apps with Gemini.

### Session Timeline

| Time | Phase | Activity |

**Task:** Build a personal "AI Workspace Playbook" that demonstrates:
- Document creation with AI (Docs)
- Collaboration and feedback (Comments)
- File organization and search (Drive)
- Meeting automation (Calendar + Meet)
- Research synthesis (Gemini)

**Deliverable:** A single document containing your 5 workflow templates.

### Peer Review Criteria

| Criteria | Weight |
|----------|--------|
| Integrates 3+ Workspace apps | 25% |
| AI assistance is meaningful | 25% |
| Workflow is repeatable | 25% |
| Documentation is clear | 25% |

**Final Artifact:** "Take your completed playbook and add ONE new workflow you want to try next week. That's your bridge to continued learning."

## Appendix: Troubleshooting Guide

| Problem | Quick Fix |
|---------|-----------|
| Gemini not appearing in app | Check google.com/settings > Gemini features |
| Slow AI responses | Explain "AI processing time - model is large" |
| Participant forgot login | Use pre-staged demo accounts |
| Off-task behavior | Redirect: "This skill will save you 30 min/week" |
| Advanced participant | Provide challenge extension tasks |
| Struggling participant | Pair with confident peer for scaffolding |
