## LEARN Phase

### Part 1: Research Workflow Fundamentals (3 minutes)

**The Traditional Research Problem**

Traditional research is time-consuming:
```
Find sources (30 min) → Read and take notes (60 min) → 
Compare and contrast (30 min) → Write summary (30 min) = 2.5 hours minimum
```

**AI-Accelerated Research:**

```
Find sources (10 min) → Paste to Gemini (2 min) → 
Prompt synthesis (3 min) → Review and refine (10 min) = 25 minutes
```

**What Gemini Does Well for Research:**

| Task | Gemini Capability | Time Saved |
|------|-------------------|------------|
| Summarize long documents | Fast extraction | 70% |
| Compare multiple sources | Side-by-side analysis | 60% |
| Generate bullet summaries | Condense to key points | 50% |
| Identify gaps | Cross-reference coverage | 40% |

**What Gemini Does NOT Replace:**

- Critical evaluation of source credibility
- Synthesis of novel insights (AI recombines, doesn't invent)
- Decision-making based on synthesized information

---

### Part 2: Gemini Synthesis Capabilities (4 minutes)

**Core Research Functions:**

1. **Extract** - Pull specific data from documents
   - "What are the key statistics in this report?"
   - "List all dates mentioned in this article"

2. **Summarize** - Condense longer content
   - "Summarize this article in 3 bullet points"
   - "What is the main argument of this paper?"

3. **Compare** - Analyze multiple sources
   - "Compare these three articles. What do they agree on? Where do they differ?"
   - "What are the conflicting viewpoints on this topic?"

4. **Generate** - Create output from synthesis
   - "Generate a one-page brief on this topic"
   - "Write an executive summary of these findings"

**Effective Research Prompts:**

| Goal | Prompt Template |
|------|-----------------|
| Extraction | "From [source], extract [specific data type]" |
| Summary | "Summarize [source] in [number] bullet points covering [topics]" |
| Comparison | "Compare [Source A] and [Source B] on [topic]. Identify agreements and conflicts." |
| Synthesis | "Based on [Source A], [Source B], [Source C], generate a [deliverable] about [topic]" |

## CONSOLIDATE Phase

### Guided Workshop: Research Synthesis Task (10 minutes)

**Task Setup:**
- Open the provided source materials folder
- You will find 3 articles on: "Remote Work Productivity"

**Task 1: Extract Key Data (3 minutes)**

1. Select the key data section from each article
2. Copy to a new Google Doc
3. Use Gemini to extract: "List all statistics mentioned in these articles with their source"

**Task 2: Synthesize Sources (4 minutes)**

1. In your research document, open Gemini
2. Use this prompt:

```
Based on these three articles about remote work productivity, generate:

1. Executive Summary (3 sentences)
2. Key Finding 1: [Title] with supporting evidence
3. Key Finding 2: [Title] with supporting evidence  
4. Key Finding 3: [Title] with supporting evidence
5. Conclusion (2 sentences)
6. Areas of Conflict (where sources disagree)

Format as a professional research brief.
```

3. Review the output

**Task 3: Evaluate and Refine (3 minutes)**

1. Read the AI-generated brief
2. Identify one area where the AI made an error or oversimplified
3. Note: "I noticed [issue] in the AI output because [reason]"
4. Refine with follow-up prompt: "Revise the section about [topic] to address [specific concern]"

## COMPRESS Phase

### Pattern Recap (1 minute)

**The ONE thing to remember:**

> "AI synthesizes at speed, but you verify for accuracy. Use Gemini to process sources fast, but apply critical thinking to the output."

**Research Quality Checklist:**
```
□ AI summary matches your understanding of source
□ Statistics are attributed to sources
□ Conflicts between sources are acknowledged
□ Conclusions follow logically from evidence
□ Novel insights go beyond mere recombination
```
