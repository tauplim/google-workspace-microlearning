## LEARN Phase

### Part 1: Google Workspace Ecosystem Overview (3 minutes)

Google Workspace is more than email. It's an integrated productivity suite designed for modern team collaboration.

**The 5 Core Applications:**

| App | Primary Purpose | AI Integration |
|-----|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Gmail** | Email communication | Summarize threads, draft replies |
| **Calendar** | Scheduling | Find meeting times, generate agendas |
| **Drive** | File storage & management | Natural language search, organize files |
| **Docs/Sheets/Slides** | Document creation | Write, analyze, design with AI |
| **Meet** | Video conferencing | Meeting notes, transcription |

**Key Concept: The Workspace Advantage**

Unlike standalone tools, Google Workspace apps:
- Share a common identity (single sign-on)
- Store files centrally in Drive
- Update automatically with new AI features
- Work seamlessly across devices

---

### Part 2: Gemini AI Integration Points (4 minutes)

Gemini is Google's AI assistant, embedded directly into Workspace apps.

**Where to Find Gemini:**

| Application | Gemini Location | Activation |
|-------------|-----------------|------------|
| Gmail | Sparkle icon in compose window | Click to activate |
| Docs | Tools menu → Gemini OR shortcut | Ctrl+Shift+M |
| Sheets | Sparkle icon in toolbar | Click to activate |
| Slides | Insert → Gemini menu | Click to activate |
| Drive | gemini.google.com | Web interface |
| Meet | Meeting notes (post-meeting) | Auto-generated |
| Calendar | Scheduling assist panel | Right sidebar |

**Three Ways Gemini Helps:**
1. **Generate** - Create content from scratch
2. **Summarize** - Condense existing information
3. **Analyze** - Extract insights from data or documents

---

### Part 3: Live Demonstration (4 minutes)

**Watch the trainer perform these actions:**

1. **Gmail Summarization**
   - Open a long email thread
   - Click Gemini sparkle icon
   - Select "Summarize this email"
   - Observe AI-generated summary

2. **Docs Content Generation**
   - Open new Google Doc
   - Press Ctrl+Shift+M
   - Prompt: "Write a one-paragraph introduction for a team newsletter about our Q3 wins"
   - Observe AI draft content

3. **Sheets Formula Assistance**
   - Open spreadsheet
   - Click Gemini in toolbar
   - Prompt: "Write a formula to calculate the average of column B, excluding zeros"
   - Observe AI-generated formula

4. **Drive Natural Language Search**
   - Open gemini.google.com
   - Type: "Find my spreadsheet about project budget from August"
   - Observe AI find the file

## COMPRESS Phase

### Pattern Recap (1 minute)

**The ONE thing to remember:**

> "Gemini is your AI workmate embedded in every Workspace app. It can generate, summarize, and analyze — and you activate it differently in each app."

**Gemini Activation Methods:**
- Gmail/Sheets/Slides: Click sparkle icon
- Docs: Ctrl+Shift+M or Tools → Gemini
- Drive: Use gemini.google.com web interface
- Calendar: Right sidebar panel
- Meet: Post-meeting auto-notes
