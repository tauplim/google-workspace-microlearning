The Meeting Problem

Meetings consume significant professional time

Metric Data
Average meetings per week8-12 for knowledge workers
Meeting time as % of workday35-40%
Employees feeling over-meeted71%
Time in productive meetings<50% of meeting time

Most meeting time is wasted on admin

Scheduling, agenda creation, note-taking = 60% of meeting prep

Where AI Can Help

BEFORE

  • Find time slots
  • Generate agenda
  • Send invites
  • Prep materials

DURING

  • AI notetaking
  • Transcription
  • Action capture
  • Translation

AFTER

  • Auto-generate notes
  • Distribute summary
  • Create follow-up tasks
  • Track decisions

Pattern Recap

"AI doesn't replace meeting productivity — it removes the administrative overhead. Use it to capture, not to think."

Meeting AI Decision Guide:

USE AI FOR:

  • Scheduling coordination
  • Agenda templating
  • Note capture
  • Follow-up drafting

USE HUMAN FOR:

  • Meeting necessity
  • Discussion facilitation
  • Sensitive feedback
  • Strategic decisions

Take-Away: Meeting Automation Script

MEETING AUTOMATION SCRIPT

1

SCHEDULE: "Find 30 min for [meeting] with [attendees]"

2

AGENDA: Gemini → "Create agenda for [meeting type]" → Copy to calendar

3

PREP: "Summarize relevant docs in Drive for [meeting]"

4

DURING: Let Meet AI capture notes (if enabled)

5

POST: Gemini extract → "Draft team update email"
