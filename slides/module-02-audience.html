Editing vs. Suggesting Modes

Editing Mode

Direct changes (no tracking)

Use when:

  • You fully own the document
  • Making minor edits
  • Working alone

Suggesting Mode

Proposed changes (tracked)

Use when:

  • Reviewing others' work
  • Suggesting improvements
  • Collaborative editing

Four Ways AI Enhances Collaboration

1

Generate Comments

AI suggests feedback where you might miss issues

2

Reply to Comments

AI helps craft diplomatic, thoughtful responses

3

Resolve Threads

AI identifies when discussions are complete

4

Summarize Activity

AI shows what changed and who did what

When AI Helps vs. Human Judgment

AI Helps With

  • Grammar and clarity suggestions
  • Fact-checking claims
  • Summarizing long threads
  • Flagging inconsistencies

Use Human Judgment

  • Tone and sensitivity
  • Strategic decisions
  • Relationship nuance
  • Creative direction

WORKSHOP

Task 1: Set Up Shared Document

Time: 2 minutes

  1. 1

    Create new Google Doc titled: "Module 2 Workshop - [Your Name]"

  2. 2

    Click the Share button

  3. 3

    Select "Anyone with the link can edit"

  4. 4

    Copy the link and send it to your neighbor

WORKSHOP

Task 2: Practice Suggesting Mode

Time: 3 minutes

  1. 1

    Switch to Suggesting mode (dropdown in toolbar)

  2. 2

    Go to your neighbor's shared document

  3. 3

    Add a suggestion to improve one sentence (appears in green)

  4. 4

    Accept or reject your neighbor's suggestion in your document

WORKSHOP

Task 3: AI-Enhanced Comments

Time: 5 minutes

  1. 1

    In your document, highlight a paragraph

  2. 2

    Click Comment icon, then Gemini sparkle

  3. 3

    Try one prompt: "Suggest improvement" / "Summarize" / "Identify unclear"

  4. 4

    Reply to a comment using AI, then Resolve the thread

Record your work in the participant workbook

PAIR EXERCISE

Peer Review with AI

A

Partner A: Share a short paragraph (3-4 sentences)

B

Partner B: Use AI to generate 2 improvement suggestions

C

Partner B: Reply using AI assistance

Discussion:

When did AI comments feel helpful? When did they feel robotic or inappropriate?

Pattern Recap

"AI makes collaboration faster but judgment still matters. Use AI for generating options, not making decisions."

The AI Collaboration Decision Tree:

Is the comment about grammar/clarity? → AI can likely help

Is the decision strategic or relational? → Use human judgment

Take-Away: AI Collaboration Checklist

Save this workflow for your next collaborative document

AI COLLABORATION CHECKLIST

PRE-WORK:

  • Enable Suggesting mode for collaborative drafting
  • Share with "Anyone can edit" for team documents
  • Add AI comment: "What should I improve here?"

DURING COLLABORATION:

  • Use AI to generate reply when unsure of tone
  • Ask AI: "Is this comment clear and constructive?"
  • Use AI to summarize unresolved comment threads

WRAP-UP:

  • Resolve completed comment threads
  • Use AI: "Summarize all remaining feedback"
  • Check version history for any lost content
