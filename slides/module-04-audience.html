Find sources30 min
Read & take notes60 min
Compare & contrast30 min
Write summary30 min

= 2.5 hours minimum

AI-Accelerated Research

Find sources10 min
Paste to Gemini2 min
Prompt synthesis3 min

What Gemini Does Well for Research

Task Gemini Capability Time Saved
Summarize long documentsFast extraction70%
Compare multiple sourcesSide-by-side analysis60%
Generate bullet summariesCondense to key points50%
Identify gapsCross-reference coverage40%

What Gemini Does NOT Replace:

  • Critical evaluation of source credibility
  • Synthesis of novel insights (AI recombines, doesn't invent)
  • Decision-making based on synthesized information

Core Research Functions

1. EXTRACT

Pull specific data from documents

"What are the key statistics in this report?"

2. SUMMARIZE

Condense longer content

"Summarize this article in 3 bullet points"

3. COMPARE

Analyze multiple sources

"Compare these three articles. What do they agree on?"

4. GENERATE

Create output from synthesis

"Generate a one-page research brief"

Effective Research Prompts

EXTRACT

Prompt:

"From [source], extract [specific data type]"

SUMMARIZE

Prompt:

"Summarize [source] in [number] bullets covering [topics]"

COMPARE

Prompt:

"Compare [A] and [B] on [topic]. Identify agreements and conflicts."

SYNTHESIZE

Prompt:

"Based on [sources], generate a [deliverable] about [topic]"

WORKSHOP

Task 1: Extract Key Data

Time: 3 minutes

  1. 1

    Select key data section from each of the 3 articles

  2. 2

    Copy to a new Google Doc

  3. 3

    Use Gemini: "List all statistics mentioned with their source"

WORKSHOP

Task 2: Synthesize Sources

Time: 4 minutes

Use this prompt in Gemini:

Based on these three articles about remote work productivity, generate:

1. Executive Summary (3 sentences)

2. Key Finding 1: [Title] with supporting evidence

3. Key Finding 2: [Title] with supporting evidence

4. Key Finding 3: [Title] with supporting evidence

5. Conclusion (2 sentences)

6. Areas of Conflict (where sources disagree)

Review the output - does it make sense?

WORKSHOP

Task 3: Evaluate and Refine

Time: 3 minutes

  1. 1

    Read the AI-generated brief carefully

  2. 2

    Identify one area where AI made an error or oversimplified

  3. 3

    Refine: "Revise the section about [topic] to address [concern]"

Pattern Recap

"AI synthesizes at speed, but you verify for accuracy. Use Gemini to process sources fast, but apply critical thinking to the output."

Research Quality Checklist:

AI summary matches your understanding of source

Statistics are attributed to sources

Conflicts between sources are acknowledged

Conclusions follow logically from evidence

Take-Away: Research Workflow

RESEARCH WORKFLOW AUTOMATION

STEP 1: COLLECT

  • Gather 3-5 relevant sources
  • Copy key sections (not entire documents)

STEP 2: EXTRACT

  • Prompt: "Extract all statistics, claims, and dates"
  • Create comparison table

STEP 3: SYNTHESIZE

  • Prompt: "Generate [deliverable] comparing sources"
  • Specify format (brief, bullets, narrative)

STEP 4: VERIFY

  • Read AI output against original sources
  • Flag errors or misrepresented claims

STEP 5: FINALIZE

  • Add citations and references
  • Note limitations and gaps
