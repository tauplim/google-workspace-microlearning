What is Prompt Chaining?

Breaking a complex task into sequential AI operations where output becomes input for the next step.

Simple vs. Chain:

Simple: One prompt → One response

Chain: Multiple steps → Compound output

Chain 1: Research → Summarize → Draft

RESEARCH → SYNTHESIZE → DRAFT

1

INPUT: 3 articles on remote work

2

STEP 1: Gemini extracts key findings from all sources

3

STEP 2: Gemini synthesizes findings into comparison

4

STEP 3: Gemini drafts executive brief from synthesis

5

OUTPUT: Ready-to-share research brief

Chain 2: Draft → Review → Refine → Send

4

Effective Chain Design Principles

1

One transformation per step

Do not ask for too much at once

2

Clear output format

Tell Gemini what format to produce

3

Verification point

Check output before proceeding

4

Error handling

Ask Gemini to flag if input is insufficient

Pattern Recap

"Prompt chaining turns AI from a one-off assistant into an automated workflow engine."

Chain Building Rules:

1

Each step does ONE thing well

2

Tell Gemini what format to output

3

Review output before next step

4

Save successful chains as templates
