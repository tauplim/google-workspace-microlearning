Requirements

The A.P.E. Framework

A

ASSESS

What trigger or input starts this workflow?

Email arrives, meeting ends, document created

P

PROCESS

What AI transformations happen?

Summarize, extract, route, draft

E

EXECUTE

What is the final output or action?

Email sent, file saved, task created

Workflow Design Checklist

□

What triggers this workflow? (input/event)

□

What Workspace apps are involved? (min 3)

□

What does AI do at each step?

□

What is the final output?

□

How long does this save me?

□
