# Prompt: Industry Expert Course Topic Research

**Task:** Research Google Workspace with AI Microlearning Course Market Opportunity

**Agent:** industry-research-expert

**Strategic Context Document Location:** `plan/research/google-workspace-stragetic-document.md`

---

## Research Objective

Research the corporate training market opportunity for a Google Workspace with AI microlearning course. This course teaches professionals how to leverage Google Workspace tools (Docs, Sheets, Gmail, Calendar, Meet, Drive) with Gemini AI to improve workplace productivity.

---

## Research Focus Areas

### 1. Market Size and Growth

- Total corporate training market size and projections
- E-learning and microlearning market segment growth (CAGR through 2030)
- AI-integrated learning solutions growth trends
- Google Workspace adoption rates in enterprise/mid-market

### 2. Competitive Landscape

- Primary competitors offering Google Workspace training
- Key players: Google Cloud Skills Boost, Coursera, Intellezy, Netskill, Microsoft Viva Learning
- Adjacent competitors and substitutes
- Differentiation opportunities for a purpose-built microlearning platform

### 3. Target Audience Demographics and Pain Points

- Primary buyer profile (job title, company size, industry)
- Learner profile (role, experience level, tech proficiency)
- Top pain points in current Google Workspace training
- Current training hour allocation per employee
- ROI measurement challenges

### 4. Pricing Models

- Mid-market seat-based pricing benchmarks ($/user/month)
- Enterprise volume pricing patterns
- Free pilot/free tier approaches
- Value-based pricing opportunities

### 5. Market Validation Signals

- Willingness to pay for AI-integrated productivity training
- Purchase decision drivers
- Implementation timeline factors
- Success metrics that matter to buyers
